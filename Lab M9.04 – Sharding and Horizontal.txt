Lab M9.04 – Sharding and Horizontal Partitioning Strategy


What is horizontal vs vertical partitioning?
Horizontal partioning: splits the data into small sections (sharding)
Vertical partioning: you have bigger sections

Define horizontal partitioning (sharding)
splits the database in smaller, independent pieces called shards.

Define vertical partitioning
you have bigger servers

Explain when each is appropriate and compare their scalability characteristics

Vertical: temporary solution but has physical limits and gets expensive
Horizontal: When data grows too much. Easier tot scale



What is the difference between sharding and indexing?
Indexing helps for searching and sharding for storage. 


How does sharding differ from indexing?
Same


When is indexing sufficient?

when you need to look for information in a large dataset.

When is sharding necessary?
When you have a big volumen of data

Can you use both together?
Yes
